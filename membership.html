5 Reasons to Become a Member of

Port Stephens FM

PORT STEPHENS FM IS A NOT FOR PROFIT, MEMBERSHIP BASED, RADIO STATION BROADCASTING TO OUR COMMUNITY AND THE GREATER PORT STEPHENS AREA.
  • looks_one
    As a community radio station we are limited in the revenue we can generate from sponsors. Revenue from our members is an important source of station financial support. All of our staff are unpaid volunteers. All membership income is used to fund station operations.
  • looks_two
    Members have the right to attend and vote at general meetings. Accordingly, you would be welcome to attend the general meetings and to contribute your thoughts regarding station operations.
  • looks_3
    As a new member you would be invited to observe a presenter for up to an hour to experience station operations, even selecting a track or two.
  • looks_4
    Your child or grandchild would be invited to record a station promotion announcement to be used On-Air.
  • looks_5
    You would be offered a birthday announcement for one of your children/ grandchildren.
