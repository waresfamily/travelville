We love hearing from listeners

Feedback

Port Stephens FM welcomes your feedback and suggestions - be it a compliment or complaint - we are all ears.

When you have something to say, we listen, so help us become an even better radio station and fill out the form below:

library_music

Request a song

You can call us between 6am and 9.00pm on 4984 6666 to talk directly with our on-air presenter if you would like to request a song. Please keep in mind that we will do our best to play your song but occasionally we will be short of time in a program and may not be able to, or if it doesn't quite fit the current program (say, a request for hard rock in a jazz program) we may have to play it for you another time, in a more suitable program.
