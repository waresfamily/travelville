Volunteers

Ever dreamed of being a radio presenter? Step out of that dream and into our studio …
If you have a clear, friendly and confident voice, a positive personality and have enough spare time each week to present a program and help out with keeping the station operating, attending events and outside broadcasts, then we would love to hear from you!
Our programs are presented by announcers from 6am and can run through to midnight each day - so there is plenty of opportunity to find a shift that suits your lifestyle.
Opportunities range from presenting daytime programmed music where you get to add value with interviews, news and features through to the specialist music programs broadcast of an evening or weekend.
Our presenters range from young to young-at-heart, so age is no barrier! We'll give you all the training you need and support you in developing your presenting skills.
If you're interested in becoming a presenter
Click here to complete our New Presenter Information Form
and we will contact you.
Maybe you're not so keen on presenting but have skills or experience in many of the other roles it takes to keep our great station broadcasting 24/7 every day of the year. We are always in need of help with office administration, website maintenance, marketing and events, newsletter production and all things technical - the list is endless.
If you're interested in becoming a NON-presenter volunteer
Click here to complete our NON-Presenter Information Form

All positions at PSFM are of a voluntary nature - so if you can donate some of your time and skills, we can offer you all the fun and work satisfaction that comes with working in a great team for a great radio station.
